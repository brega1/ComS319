Bryce Rega. Cat Lover.

Bryce is a Junior at Iowa State from Excelsior, Minnesota. When he's not working on school work, Bryce is often working on Iowa State's solar car by upgrading and managing its custom software.

Throughout much of Bryce's childhood, he had a cat to hang out with. This cat was named China because she was as small as a China doll when she was rescued. She was older than Bryce and lived a long life. Now Bryce wants to share his love for cats he experienced as a child with anyone else he can.

Gavin Patel. Dog Lover.

Gavin is a junior originally from Park Ridge, Illinois. Through his time studying at Iowa State, he has found an interest in embedded software development, which he interned for at John Deere.

Throughout Gavin's life, pets have been a key part of his life. Currently, he has a dog named Spanky, whom he adopted when he was in middle school. He is super excited to be able to share his passion and love for pets with anyone he can.
